A gymnasium

Admin Web Design 50 Comments

A gymnasium, also known as a gym, is a covered location for athletics The word is derived from the ancient Greek term gymnasium,They are commonly found in athletic and fitness centres, and as activity and learning spaces in educational institutions.

History of Gym

Admin Web Design 50 Comments

The first gymnasiums in history were dated over 3000 years ago, in ancient Persia, where they were known as Zurkhaneh, as areas that encouraged physical fitness, Gyms were usually a locality for the education of young men, which included physical education and bathing and studying.
